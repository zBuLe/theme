## The Pursuit of Temporal Geology 

The **Threan Institute of Temporal Geology** was founded on a simple, irrefutable premise: *rocks are simply water that has lost its motivation.* By applying advanced aerodynamic principles to completely stationary boulders, we have unlocked the secrets of the subterranean cosmos.

### Core Disciplines

Our research is divided into three primary atmospheric phenomena, each requiring absolute dedication and a fundamental misunderstanding of basic physics:

* **Acoustic Silence:** Measuring the exact decibel level of thoughts before they are spoken.
    * *Sub-discipline:* The echo of forgotten dreams in empty corridors.
* **Liquid Geometry:** Attempting to force rivers to recognize the authority of a right angle.
* **Combustible Ice:** Because standard fire is simply too predictable and lacks structural integrity.

### Operational Procedures

To initiate a temporal geological scan, field operatives must strictly adhere to the following sequence:

1. Approach the target specimen (usually a very confused badger or a small, unassuming pebble).
2. Recite the foundational axioms of zero-gravity subterranean farming.
3. Inject the following configuration parameters into the simulation terminal:

```json
{
  "dimension": "pocket",
  "gravity_coefficient": "strictly_optional",
  "tea_temperature": "earl_grey_hot",
  "target_coordinates": [ null, "slightly_left", 42 ]
}

```

If the `target_coordinates` fail to initialize, you must manually verify the `chronosphere.exe` module and verbally apologize to the nearest piece of quartz.
